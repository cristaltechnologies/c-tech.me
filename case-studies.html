Success Story 02
WMS implementation for a high-volume FMCG distribution operation
Regional FMCG distributor — UAE
WMS INFOR WMS FMCG Distribution
The Challenge

A high-volume FMCG distributor across the UAE was managing warehouse operations through manual processes and a legacy system that could no longer support the speed and accuracy demands of the business. Order fulfilment errors, inventory discrepancies, and slow receiving were generating customer complaints and increasing operational cost.

What We Delivered

Cristal Technologies designed and implemented a Warehouse Management System (WMS) solution covering the full inbound and outbound cycle — receiving, put-away, replenishment, pick-and-pack, and dispatch with carrier integration. Process design was conducted with warehouse supervisors before configuration began, ensuring the system matched how the warehouse actually operated.

Outcomes
Reduced pick errors Faster receiving cycle Real-time inventory visibility Carrier dispatch integration Supervisor-designed workflows
Industry: FMCG Distribution
Platform: INFOR WMS
Scope: Inbound · Storage · Replenishment · Outbound · Dispatch
Region: UAE
Success Story 03
BI consolidation and executive dashboards for an FMCG distributor post-ERP migration
FMCG distributor — Gulf region
BI Business Intelligence Healthcare Trade Post-migration
The Challenge

Following a JD Edwards implementation, a leading FMCG distributor in the Gulf found its management reporting had fragmented — data spread across the new ERP, a legacy system still in partial use, and several manual spreadsheets. Finance and commercial teams were spending days each week producing reports that were already two or three days old by the time they reached management.

What We Delivered

Cristal Technologies built a consolidated BI layer connecting the JD Edwards environment to a centralised reporting platform, with executive dashboards covering gross margin by branch, inventory aging, sales vs. target, and supplier spend. The manual reconciliation process was eliminated — management now receives daily automated reports and on-demand dashboard access.

Outcomes
Daily automated reporting Manual extraction eliminated Gross margin by branch Inventory aging dashboard Single source of truth
Industry: FMCG Distribution
Platforms: Oracle JD Edwards · Power BI
Scope: Data consolidation · Executive dashboards · Automated reporting
Region: UAE
