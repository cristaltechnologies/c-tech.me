Company Overview

Who We Are

Cristal Technologies is a specialist enterprise IT consultancy delivering senior, vendor-neutral advisory and hands-on implementation across Oracle & Microsoft ERP, INFOR Warehouse Management Systems, and Business Intelligence platforms built on Power BI and Cognos.

Founded and led by Samir Harb, the practice is built on 40 years of enterprise operations experience. An FMCG domain expert with extensive B2B experience, Samir has worked with leading global brands including P&G, Mars, Nestlé, and Clorox — bringing deep commercial and operational knowledge to every engagement.

Based between Dubai and Beirut, Cristal Technologies brings direct regional knowledge to every engagement, including deep familiarity with the regulatory and operational realities of the Gulf and Levant corridor.

Our Mission

To deliver senior, vendor-neutral enterprise technology advisory that helps FMCG, distribution, financial services, and healthcare trade businesses across the Gulf and Levant achieve operational excellence.

Our Approach

Every engagement begins with the business problem — not the system. We work backwards from the outcome the client needs, delivered by the same senior consultant who scoped the project. No junior handoffs. No vendor agenda.
