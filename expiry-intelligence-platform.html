Integrations

Integrates with most ERPs

The Expiry Intelligence Platform connects to your existing ERP — no rework of your systems required. Data is pulled directly from your item master and lot/batch tables.

Oracle JD Edwards
F4101 Item Master, F4108 Lot Master & F41021 Location Detail via direct DB or JDBC connector.
Microsoft Dynamics 365
Item tracking, batch numbers & inventory journals via OData REST API or direct SQL on Business Central / F&O.
SAP ERP / S/4HANA
MARA, MCHB & MARD tables for material master, batch stock & storage location data via RFC or HANA views.
INFOR & Other ERPs
INFOR M3/LN, Odoo, Epicor, and custom ERP systems via CSV export, REST API, or direct database connection.

No ERP changes required. The platform reads directly from your existing item master and lot tables — your systems stay untouched.
